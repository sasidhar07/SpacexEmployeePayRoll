About the Kennedy Space Center SpaceX Launch

  • Launch Date: 26 February 2023
  • Launch Time: 02:07 AM (EST)
  • Location: Kennedy Space Center Launch Complex 39b, Florida
  • Viewing Areas: Apollo/Saturn V Center and surrounding areas
  • Astronauts: Stephen Bowen (Mission Commander), Woody Hoburg (Pilot), Sultan Allneyadi (Flight Engineer), and Andrey Fedyaev (Mission Specialist)
